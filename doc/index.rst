Welcome to dino's documentation!
================================

Dino is a modern DNS record editor for PowerDNS. It uses the PowerDNS-API, has a
high test coverage, rich documentation and comes with batteries included!

Take a look at the documentation sections on the left side and choose something
to your liking.
